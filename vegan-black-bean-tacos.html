Vegan Black Bean Tacos with Mango Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 jalape√±o, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 avocado, sliced

Instructions

  1. Make the mango salsa: combine diced mango, red onion, jalape√±o, lime juice, and cilantro. Season with salt and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add black beans, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. Cook for 5-7 minutes, mashing some beans with a fork for texture. Add a splash of water if needed.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame.
  5. Assemble tacos: spiced black beans, mango salsa, sliced avocado. Squeeze extra lime on top and serve immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use canned mango?

Fresh is best for salsa, but thawed frozen mango works in a pinch.

How do I store leftover beans?

Store seasoned beans separately from salsa. They keep 4-5 days in the fridge.

What can I use instead of mango?

Pineapple or peach salsa are great alternatives.
