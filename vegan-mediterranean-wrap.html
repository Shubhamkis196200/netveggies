Mediterranean Hummus Wrap

Delicious mediterranean hummus wrap that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, toss cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and parsley with lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet for 30 seconds each side to make them pliable.
  3. Spread 1/4 cup hummus across the center of each tortilla.
  4. Spoon the vegetable mixture on top of the hummus.
  5. Fold the bottom edge up, then fold in the sides and roll tightly into a wrap.
  6. Slice in half diagonally and serve immediately, or wrap in foil for a packed lunch.

  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
  • Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices.
