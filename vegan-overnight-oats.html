Overnight Oats ‚Äî 5 Flavor Variations

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup plant milk (oat, almond, or soy)
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, plant milk, yogurt, chia seeds, maple syrup, and vanilla in a jar or container.
  2. Stir well, seal, and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours).
  3. In the morning, stir and add your chosen toppings. Enjoy cold or warm it up!
  4. VARIATION 1 ‚Äî Peanut Butter Banana: Add 1 tbsp PB + sliced banana + cinnamon.
  5. VARIATION 2 ‚Äî Berry Bliss: Add mixed berries + coconut flakes.
  6. VARIATION 3 ‚Äî Chocolate: Add 1 tbsp cocoa powder + chocolate chips.
  7. VARIATION 4 ‚Äî Apple Pie: Add diced apple + cinnamon + walnuts.
  8. VARIATION 5 ‚Äî Tropical: Add mango + pineapple + shredded coconut.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do overnight oats last?

They keep in the fridge for up to 5 days, making them perfect for weekly meal prep.

Can I eat them warm?

Yes! Microwave for 1-2 minutes or heat on the stove with a splash of extra milk.

Are overnight oats healthy?

Yes ‚Äî they're packed with fiber, complex carbs, and healthy fats. Adding chia seeds boosts protein and omega-3s.
