Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Delicious quinoa stuffed bell peppers that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers, tops cut off and seeded
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Place hollowed peppers upright in a baking dish.
  2. Mix cooked rice, black beans, corn, 3/4 cup salsa, cumin, chili powder, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stuff each pepper generously with the rice mixture, pressing down gently.
  4. Spoon remaining salsa over the tops of the stuffed peppers.
  5. Cover with foil and bake 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes until peppers are tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with avocado or vegan sour cream.
