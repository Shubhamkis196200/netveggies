Thai Peanut Noodles (15 Minutes)

Quick and creamy Thai-inspired peanut noodles with veggies. Ready in 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz rice noodles or spaghetti
    • \n
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
    • \n
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
    • \n
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
    • \n
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
    • \n
  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
    • \n
  • 1 cup edamame
    • \n
  • Crushed peanuts and cilantro for garnish

  • Calories: 420

  • Fiber: 8g

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
    2. \n
  2. In a bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, maple syrup, sriracha, and garlic until smooth.
    3. \n
  3. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper, cabbage, and edamame. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
    4. \n
  4. Add cooked noodles and peanut sauce. Toss to combine and heat through.
    5. \n
  5. Serve topped with crushed peanuts and fresh cilantro.
