No-Bake Energy Balls (5 Ways)

Delicious no-bake energy balls (5 ways) that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Stir until everything is well combined.
  2. If mixture is too dry, add a splash of almond milk. If too wet, add more oats.
  3. Refrigerate the mixture for 15-20 minutes so it firms up and is easier to roll.
  4. Using your hands, roll into 1-inch balls (should make about 18-20 balls).
  5. Place on a parchment-lined tray and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 1 week, or freeze for up to 3 months.

  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
  • Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices.
