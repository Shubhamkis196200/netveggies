Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Introduction

NetVeggies ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website netveggies.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Newsletter Subscriptions: When you sign up for our newsletter, we collect your email address and name (if provided).
  • Contact Forms: When you contact us, we collect your name, email address, and any information you include in your message.
  • Comments: If you leave comments on recipes, we may collect your name, email address, and the content of your comment.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience on our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes information like pages visited, time spent on site, and general location (city/country level).
  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information including your IP address, browser type, referring URLs, and timestamps.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our weekly newsletter (if you've subscribed)
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer service requests
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Understand how visitors use our site through analytics
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our website (email service providers, analytics providers, hosting providers).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Third-Party Services

Our site may use third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze site traffic. Google Privacy Policy
  • Email Service Providers: To send newsletters and updates.
  • Affiliate Links: We participate in the Amazon Associates program. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Understanding site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improving site functionality

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of our site may not function properly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal obligations).
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of our email newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

External Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@netveggies.com
  • Contact Form: netveggies.com/contact

Your Privacy Matters

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We will never sell your data to third parties, and we only collect information necessary to provide you with the best possible experience on NetVeggies.
