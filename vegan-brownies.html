Fudgy Vegan Brownies

Delicious fudgy vegan brownies that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g

  • Fat: 10g

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Stir until just combined ‚Äî don't overmix.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips. Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 22-25 minutes until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs (not wet batter).
  6. Let cool completely in the pan before slicing into 9 squares.
