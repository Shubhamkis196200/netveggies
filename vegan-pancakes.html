Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

Delicious fluffy vegan pancakes that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups almond milk
  • 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Mix almond milk with apple cider vinegar in a measuring cup. Let sit 5 minutes to create vegan buttermilk.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the buttermilk mixture, melted coconut oil, and vanilla to dry ingredients. Stir until just combined (lumps are fine!).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with coconut oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form on top and edges look set (about 2-3 minutes).
  6. Flip and cook 1-2 more minutes until golden. Serve with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or vegan butter.

  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
  • Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices.
