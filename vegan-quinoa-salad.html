Lemon Herb Quinoa Salad

Delicious lemon herb quinoa salad that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Bring quinoa and water to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes until water is absorbed.
  2. Fluff quinoa with a fork and let cool to room temperature (or spread on a baking sheet to cool faster).
  3. In a large bowl, combine cooled quinoa, black beans, corn, bell pepper, red onion, and cilantro.
  4. Whisk together lime juice, olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. Pour dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Taste and adjust seasonings.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate ‚Äî this salad tastes even better after a few hours. Keeps 4 days refrigerated.

  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
  • Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices.
