Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious chewy chocolate chip cookies that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup coconut oil, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together softened coconut oil, brown sugar, and white sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add almond milk and vanilla extract to the wet mixture. Mix well.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls onto baking sheets, spacing 2 inches apart. Bake 10-12 minutes until golden edges.
  7. Cool on baking sheet 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.
