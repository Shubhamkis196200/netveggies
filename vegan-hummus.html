Creamy Homemade Hummus

Delicious creamy homemade hummus that's easy to make and full of flavor.

‚è±Ô∏è 30 min üçΩÔ∏è 4 servings üå± Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2-3 tbsp water
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Smoked paprika for garnish

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Add chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, cumin, and salt to a food processor.
  2. Process for 2 minutes, scraping down sides once. The mixture will be thick at first.
  3. With the processor running, stream in water one tablespoon at a time until silky smooth.
  4. Taste and adjust: more lemon for tang, more tahini for richness, more salt as needed.
  5. Transfer to a serving bowl, create a swirl with a spoon, drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle smoked paprika.
  6. Serve with pita bread, fresh vegetables, or use as a sandwich spread. Keeps refrigerated 5-7 days.

üí° Pro Tips

  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
  • Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices.
