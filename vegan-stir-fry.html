Quick Veggie Stir Fry

Delicious quick veggie stir fry that's easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water
  • Cooked rice for serving

Nutrition

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 10g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Press tofu for 15 minutes, then cut into 3/4-inch cubes. Toss with 1 tbsp soy sauce.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat. Add tofu and cook 5-6 minutes until golden on all sides. Remove.
  3. In the same pan, stir-fry broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Return tofu to pan. Add remaining soy sauce, rice vinegar, and the cornstarch slurry.
  6. Toss everything together for 1-2 minutes until sauce thickens and coats the vegetables. Serve over rice.

  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
  • Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices.
