Frequently Asked Questions

Can you create a custom recipe for me?

While we can't create custom recipes for individuals, we absolutely take requests into consideration for future content! If there's a dish you'd love to see veganized or a specific cooking challenge you're facing, let us know and we'll add it to our recipe development queue.

I made one of your recipes and it didn't turn out. What went wrong?

We're so sorry to hear that! Every oven, stove, and kitchen is different, so sometimes adjustments are needed. Please reach out with details about what happened (was it too thick? Too thin? Didn't cook through?) and we'll troubleshoot with you. We want you to succeed!

Can I share your recipes on my blog or social media?

We love when you share our recipes! You're welcome to share a photo and a brief description with a link back to the original recipe on our site. Do you accept sponsored content or brand partnerships?

We're selective about partnerships, but we're open to working with brands that align with our values of accessible, affordable, plant-based cooking. If you represent a brand and would like to discuss a collaboration, please use the contact form above and select "Business/Partnership Inquiry."

I found a typo or error on your site.

Thank you for letting us know! We strive for accuracy but we're human. Please send us the details and we'll fix it right away.

How do I sign up for your newsletter?

You'll find newsletter signup forms throughout the site, or you can scroll to the bottom of this page to sign up. We send one email per week with new recipes, meal planning tips, and exclusive content for subscribers.
