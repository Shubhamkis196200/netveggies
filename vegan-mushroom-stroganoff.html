Creamy Mushroom Stroganoff

Rich and creamy mushroom stroganoff over pasta. Comfort food at its finest.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz mushrooms, sliced
    • \n
  • 1 large onion, diced
    • \n
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp flour
    • \n
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
    • \n
  • 1 cup cashew cream (1/2 cup cashews blended with 1 cup water)
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
    • \n
  • 2 tsp paprika
    • \n
  • 12 oz pasta
    • \n
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • \n
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Nutrition

  • Calories: 480
  • Protein: 16g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Carbs: 68g
  • Fat: 18g

Per serving. Values are estimates.

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
    2. \n
  2. In a large pan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms and cook until browned, 8-10 minutes.
    3. \n
  3. Add onion and garlic. Cook for 3-4 minutes until soft.
    4. \n
  4. Sprinkle flour over mushrooms and stir for 1 minute.
    5. \n
  5. Slowly add vegetable broth, stirring constantly. Add cashew cream, mustard, and paprika.
    6. \n
  6. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until thickened. Season with salt and pepper.
    7. \n
  7. Toss with cooked pasta, adding reserved pasta water if needed. Garnish with parsley.

  • This recipe is freezer-friendly! Freeze portions for quick meals later.
