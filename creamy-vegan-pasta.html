Creamy Vegan Pasta with Roasted Garlic

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta (penne, fusilli, or rigatoni)
  • 1 cup raw cashews (soaked 2 hours or boiled 15 min)
  • 1 whole head garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400¬∞F. Cut top off garlic head, drizzle with olive oil, wrap in foil, and roast for 35 minutes until golden and soft.
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  3. Blend soaked cashews, roasted garlic (squeezed from skins), vegetable broth, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, salt and pepper until silky smooth.
  4. Toss drained pasta with cashew cream sauce. Add pasta water a splash at a time until desired consistency.
  5. Serve topped with fresh basil, cracked pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make this nut-free?

Yes! Replace cashews with raw sunflower seeds or use silken tofu for the sauce base.

How do I store leftovers?

Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days. Add a splash of plant milk when reheating.

Can I use a different pasta shape?

Absolutely. Any short pasta works great ‚Äî penne, rigatoni, shells, or even spaghetti.
